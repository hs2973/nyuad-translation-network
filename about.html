About Translation Network

Translation Network is space for members of the NYUAD community to experiment with translation - to share original writing in any language and to translate one another's writing into any language. This platform encourages writing across linguistic, geographic, and disciplinary boundaries, in appreciating of the many languages spoken and studied at NYU Abu Dhabi and at NYU's global locations. Through the medium of translation, we hope that students, faculty, and staff will gain a portal to share their experience with one another, no matter where they might find themselves around the world.


A team of curators is responsible for reviewing all contributions submitted to the network before posting them to the webpage. If you are interested in participating as a curator, let us know at nyuad.translationnetwork@nyu.edu.
